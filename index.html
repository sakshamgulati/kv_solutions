Executive Summary

KV Solutions is pleased to submit this proposal for the implementation of a boutique, health-focused vending network at Line 5 Condos. Our mission is to replace the outdated "junk food" vending model with a curated, "Green" retail experience that mirrors the wellness-oriented lifestyle of your residents.

This is a zero-cost, zero-risk partnership. KV Solutions handles 100% of the equipment, installation, and daily operations. Your building receives a premium resident amenity at no capital cost and no management burden whatsoever.
02

The KV Solutions Philosophy: "Healthy & Green"

Modern Toronto residents — particularly along the Yonge & Eglinton corridor — are increasingly health-conscious. Traditional vending machines are no longer an amenity; they are a relic.

  • Healthier Options First: Less sugar, more protein, fewer artificial additives.
  • Dietary Variety: Plant-based, high-protein, and lower-calorie choices.
  • Environmentally Conscious: Recyclable packaging and energy-efficient hardware.
  • Local Sourcing: Ontario-based brands where available.
03

Why Line 5 Condos?

Line 5 Condos represents one of Midtown Toronto's newest luxury developments — nearly 900 suites across two towers, steps from Eglinton Subway and the new Crosstown LRT. Residents are high-earning young professionals who demand convenience without compromising on health.

We target two high-value moments in the building:

  1. The Performance Moment (Fitness Center): Residents seeking protein, electrolytes, or post-workout recovery fuel.
  2. The Focus Moment (Co-working Lounge): Remote workers and professionals seeking clean caffeine and sustained energy.

What the Building Gets

06

Operations & Equipment

Dimensions Approx. 72" H × 35" W × 35" D (6 ft × 3 ft × 3 ft)
Floor Space ~10–15 sq ft per unit including service clearance
Electricity ~$40–55/month per unit at Ontario rates. Drawn from existing common area outlets — standard industry practice. Hydro offset arrangement available on request.
Stocking Weekly remote-monitored restocking, with sanitization of touch surfaces on every visit.
Uptime 24–48 hour resolution window for any mechanical issues via remote monitoring.
Resident Feedback QR code on each machine links to a feedback form. Inventory adjusted monthly based on resident input.
